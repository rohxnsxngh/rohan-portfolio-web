# Quantum Computing Optimization Research

An investigation into quantum computing algorithms for solving complex optimization problems, with a focus on practical applications in the NISQ (Noisy Intermediate-Scale Quantum) era.

## Research Overview

Our research explores the potential of quantum computing to solve optimization problems that are intractable for classical computers. We focus on developing hybrid quantum-classical algorithms that can work effectively on current quantum hardware.
